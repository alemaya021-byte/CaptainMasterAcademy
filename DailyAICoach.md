# Daily AI Coach

## Purpose

The Daily AI Coach converts readiness data into a practical study mission for the current day.

## Generated Output

The coach generates:

- Today's Mission
- Today's priorities
- Questions to answer
- Incident simulations to complete
- Flashcards to review
- Estimated study time
- Estimated readiness improvement

## Recommendation Inputs

The coach considers:

- Overall readiness score
- Weak topics
- Forgotten material
- Missed-question queue
- Overdue flashcards
- Incident readiness profile
- Flashcard due count

## Behavior

When readiness is high, the coach emphasizes maintenance, exam-speed practice, and command polish.

When readiness is low or developing, the coach emphasizes weak chapters, forgotten material, missed-question recovery, and higher-volume practice.
